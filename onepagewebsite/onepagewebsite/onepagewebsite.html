Haldiram’s Foods International Pvt Ltd

Haldiram’s is an Indian Sweet, Snacks, and Restaurants Company. It is a well-known company in the food industry and founded by Ganga Bhishan Agarwal. Ṭhe Haldiram based out of the Dehli and Nagpur and maintains its own retail store chain as well as restaurants in Nagpur, Dehli, Kolkata, Noida, and Pune.

History

The Haldiram’s was founded in 1937 by Ganga Bishan Agarwal (Haldiram’s) at Bikaner, Rajasthan. That period of time Haldiram’s mostly known for retail Sweet and Namkeen Shop. This shop gained popularity in a short period of time, due to its unique testing Bhujia. As per the Market gained, they started expansion drives for their production or increasing manufacturing capacity. Haldiram’s first manufacturing plant was started in Kolkata, West Bengal. After the huge response of their food products, they also invested in one another manufacturing factories in different regions.

Haldiram adopted the Haldiram’ Bhujiawala trademarks in 1941. Bhushan wants to expand its business to other cities like Kolkata, Nagpur, Noida, etc. In 1972, they filed an application for registration of Haldiram’s unit all over India. Nagpur manufacturing plant is the largest plant of Haldiram’s in India.

Under the family management, Bishan son Moolchand was carrying the business in all the territories, except West Bengal. In the 1980s another son of Bishan, Rameshwarlal, and his son Prabhu Shankar allowed them to establish their business for some Products by using Haldiram’s trademarks in Kolkata, West Bengal. In 2003, the company also began the convenience food (ready to eat) processing unit to capture the foodie consumers.

Product

Haldiram has 400 plus Products in different segments like sweets, snacks, cookies, Nankeen, sherbets, Pickles, etc. The company has food convenience or frozen food (ready-to-eat food) units in metro cities like Nagpur, Pune, Bangalore, and Hyderabad. Haldiram’s restaurant-packed snacks contributed 80% of sales for the Market leader. They maintain their own retail store supply line at various retail locations. Haldiram’s food products sell in approximately 80 countries all over the world. Bhujia’s contribution is more than other products. All the products go through food safety measures and some testing processes for human safety. The Haldiram Brand has grown up at a huge amount throughout the past few years. In 2017, it was crowned as the largest snack company in India. They surpassed all other domestic and international competitors in the snacks Nankeen sweet segment. Advertisement- Haldiram’s crossed the 1 billion sales mark in India and their business doubled over the past four years.

Haldiram has been winning many awards and recognization certificates. Haldiram got the International Food Award from TROFEO by International Alimentation of Barcelona, Spain (1999). The Chairman of Haldiram’s group Shivkishan Ji Agarwal received the ‘Vidharbh Gaurav Puraskar|’.
